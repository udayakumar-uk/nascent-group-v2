About Us

Dear Esteemed Clients, Business Partners, Friends, and Colleagues,

Welcome to the Nascent Group!

Our mission is to redefine the insurance servicing space with cutting-edge technology, deep-rooted expertise, and unwavering service excellence.

We embarked on this journey not to replicate the past but to break new ground, addressing market pain points with unparalleled efficiency, independence, and proven expertise.

Our tech-forward approach empowers us to tackle industry challenges in innovative ways, always staying at the forefront of what's possible. Through our sister company, H2 Group, we have access to advanced technology that our seasoned team leverages to enhance agility and add significant value to our services.

While our industry knowledge and investment in technology drive efficiency and client success, our true strength lies in our people. I am both humbled and proud to have assembled a team of incredibly talented and dedicated individuals from all around the world who share my vision and enthusiasm. Each of them brings extensive knowledge and a passion for making a difference.

Our entrepreneurial spirit fuels our ability to offer tailored solutions, adapt to changing needs, and go the extra mile to deliver market-leading service. We understand that every client and business case is unique, and our ability to tailor every relationship sets us apart from other providers.

At Nascent, we embrace high expectations and promise uncompromising service. We hold ourselves accountable for your success, continuously evaluating our performance based on your achievements. When you win, we win. It’s that simple.

Thank you for considering Nascent Group. We look forward to building a successful and enduring partnership with you.

Yours truly,

Andre Perez
Founder & CEO
