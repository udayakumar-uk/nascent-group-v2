Cat Bonds

From set-up to termination,Nascent Group will provide a full catalog of Cat Bond administration services to streamline the process for you

Set-up
  • Act as a key member of the working group, collaborating with the arranger, sponsor, counsel and other service providers
  • Assist with the incorporation and licensing of the Special Purpose Insurer and the Purpose Trust as shareholder of the SPI.
  • Provide Resident Director/s for the company
  • Assist in selecting and hiring service providers.
  • Review all documentation from the issuer's perspective.
  • Establish trust and bank accounts as needed.
  • Support the licensing application process.
  • Prepare the expense budget and financial forecasts.
Administration
  • Ensure that the company meets all the obligations per the transaction documents.
  • Coordinate and liaise with all third parties as required and in line with service agreements.
  • Oversee the secure data site, ensuring all relevant information is accessible to investors and other interested parties.
  • Coordinate the receipt of premiums and payment of coupons.
Financial Reporting
  • Keep financial records up to date.
  • Track expense payments in relation to the budget.
  • Produce Annual Financial Statements for the Shareholder and Insurance Regulator.
  • Prepare and submit annual Statutory Financial Returns.
Regulatory Matters
  • Act in roles mandated by regulations (such as Principal Representative, Segregated Account Representative, where applicable).
  • Advocate for client's interests with regulatory authorities.
  • Ensure ongoing adherence to statutory obligations, including local policies, guidelines, and codes of conduct.
  • Perform all required statutory filings.
Termination
  • Track commutation/termination terms to guarantee prompt and orderly distribution of capital and coupons to investors.
  • Ready the company for dissolution, including terminating the insurance license, releasing charges, and settling obligations.
  • Support in selecting and appointing a liquidator, and providing all required information to them.
