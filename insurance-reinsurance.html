Insurance Management

The Nascent Group offers a variety of insurance management services to insurers, reinsurers, sidecars, transformers, and catastrophe bonds. Our technologically-enabled team is inherently agile and equipped with the tools to offer efficient and expedited service.

Insurance/
Reinsurance

Starting from the pre-incorporation phase, we are equipped to offer our expertise to businesses and investors planning to start insurance/reinsurance operations in Bermuda.

Set-up and Licensing
  • Advise in ownership structure options and liaise with Nascent Corporate Services to incorporate the vehicle.
  • Assist in identifying and retaining service providers.
  • Assist in developing a budget.
  • Assist in drafting a business plan and license submission to be presented to the regulator.
Operations
  • Keep accurate books and records.
  • Coordinate with external service providers to guarantee quality service delivery.
  • Manage the opening and administration of necessary bank accounts.
  • Handle payments following established authorizations.
  • Supply resident director(s) when needed and communicate with board members on pertinent issues.
Regulatory Matters
  • Act in roles required by regulation (e.g., Principal Representative, Segregated Account Representative, as relevant).
  • Advocate for clients' interests with the regulator.
  • Ensure ongoing adherence to statutory obligations, including local policies, guidance, and codes of conduct.
  • Execute all necessary statutory filings.
Accounting Services
  • Keep financial records up to date.
  • Generate periodic management accounts.
  • Create annual financial statements for shareholders.
  • Prepare and submit annual Statutory Financial Returns and Statements.
  • Collaborate with auditors, actuaries, and regulatory authorities regarding annual audited financial statements.
  • Work with the client's headquarter finance team for necessary data for group consolidation and reporting.
Insurance Contract Administration
  • Administer (re)insurance agreements, including premium collection and claims settlement, as per contract terms.
  • Communicate with (re)insurance counterparties for timely information gathering.
  • Identify suitable accounting entries for (re)insurance transactions.
  • Implement loss reserving standards as instructed by the company’s board.
  • Coordinate with actuaries, underwriters, and loss reserve specialists as needed.
  • Conduct analysis of underwriting activities when necessary.
Treasury
  • Oversee collateral requirements, set up Trust accounts, and adjust funding as needed.
  • Acquire and process periodic investment reports for company reporting.
  • Prepare and assess cash flow projections and needs.
  • Evaluate the company’s investment administrator’s periodic reports and ensure adherence to investment guidelines and parameters.
  • Execute investment transactions based on established authorizations.
