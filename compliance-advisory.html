Compliance Advisory

Nascent Group Compliance offers cost-effective regulatory compliance consulting services and solutions so you can focus on your business requirements whilst we ensure you remain compliant.

Services

Drafting & Review of Policies & Procedures

We can help you update existing Policies and Procedures to ensure compliance with your regulatory obligations or prepare new Policies and Procedures in support of your business operations and risk profile.

AML Audits

We can ensure you meet the AML Audit annual requirement in accordance with Regulation 17(A) and provide you with a Report that will satisfy regulatory standards.

AML Compliance Support

Whether it be assistance preparing for a prudential on-site visit from the BMA or assisting with any remediation points post visit or ensuring your AML/ATF Manual is fully complaint, we will be your trusted advisor.

Corporate Governance

We can help you build a strong and effective corporate governance policy to support your business and Board of Directors.

Independent Director Role

Alison Morrison, Managing Director has acted as an independent director for a variety of regulated institutions in Bermuda and provides over 25 years of experience and knowledge. With a strong risk and compliance background, Alison will add value to any Board.

License Applications

We will help guide you through the regulatory requirements and provide support and local knowledge to make this a smooth process.

Re/Insurance Advisory

Our proficiency in underwriting combines broad-based business knowledge with detailed analysis of specific treaty exposures. We conduct thorough audits of underwriting practices and carefully examine reinsurance underwriting guidelines.
