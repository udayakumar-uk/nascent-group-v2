Services

Share Allocation Audit

For ILS Fund Managers managing various funds, we offer an independent evaluation to both investors and the Fund Manager. This assessment ensures that the allocation process for bound share contracts in reinsurance aligns with the stipulations in the offering documents.

Risk Profile Audit

For ILS Fund Managers whose funds are structured according to specific risk characteristics or objective metrics, we offer an independent analysis for both investors and the Fund Manager. This service verifies that the makeup of a particular Fund aligns with the guidelines outlined in the offering documents.

Accounting Audits

We are capable of evaluating a client's financial accounts and records to ensure their accurate representation based on specific criteria, while also verifying compliance with established standards and policies.

Company Start-Up Advice

Our impartial approach to jurisdictional preferences enables us to offer tailored start-up advice, focusing solely on your interests, be it in Bermuda, Cayman or other global locations. Additionally, we offer insights into various critical aspects of company formation to meet our client's specific requirements.

Financial Control Review And Design

To minimize errors and enhance efficiency, we provide thorough reviews of financial controls and their design, ensuring confidence in the established control processes and procedures.

Other

Our team is well-versed in devising solutions for a variety of practical issues, whether they are persistent or temporary, obligatory or hypothetical. We are prepared to tackle both emerging challenges and longstanding concerns, collaborating with you to create a customized and considered approach.

Compliance Advisory

We offer a comprehensive suite of compliance services, ensuring businesses adhere to legal standards and best practices. This suite of services is integral for companies aiming to maintain high standards of regulatory compliance and operational integrity.
